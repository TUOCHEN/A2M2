    Minuses: we don't have the project action plan and risk management. Therefore, we may be facing some problem in the team. Such as we have the ambition to achieve certain goals. But some member did not follow our action plan for the project. first, Unequal Participation that while in a group some of the members may not do that much work, while others may work hard.  Second, Time consuming: The whole process could take the time to making the decision while working in a group.

    Interesting: the major lesion our group learn on this team that is trying to build the project action plan and risk management and each person must follow the certain rule. Moreover, we need to learn how group work could be more productively.
